Deck Equipment

We specialize in repairing and maintaining all types of deck equipment on your yacht. Whether you need routine maintenance or major repairs, our team of experienced technicians has the knowledge and expertise to get the job done right.
At our Deck Equipment service, we understand the importance of having reliable and efficient deck equipment while out at sea. That's why we use only the highest quality parts and tools to ensure that your equipment is operating at its best. We also offer regular maintenance services to help prevent future problems and keep your equipment in top condition.

    Our Deck Equipment services include:
  • Windlass and anchor system repair: We can repair and replace any issues with your yacht's windlass and anchor system, ensuring that you can safely and easily anchor your yacht.
  • Davit and crane system repair: Our technicians can repair and replace any issues with your yacht's davit and crane system, ensuring that you can safely launch and retrieve your tender or other watercraft.
  • Deck winch and furling system repair: We can repair and replace any issues with your yacht's deck winch and furling system, ensuring that you can safely and efficiently control your sails.
  • Tender and lift system repair: If you have a tender or other watercraft onboard, we can repair and replace any issues with your yacht's tender and lift system, ensuring that you can safely launch and retrieve your watercraft.
